These are the spots that don't appear at the top of Google because they've

H2: What to Look for in a Hidden Gem Eatery

rather than many things adequately. A short menu signals confidence and
quality control.

H3: Fast service without the rush
Hidden gems are usually efficient without feeling like a conveyor belt.
You're in and out fast, but you never feel rushed.
