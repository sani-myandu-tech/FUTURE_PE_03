FUTURE_PE_03 – SEO PROMPT ENGINEERING — FULL PROMPT SUITE

 PROMPT 1: ADVANCED PILLAR BLOG PROMPT
Role:
  Act as a senior SEO strategist and conversion-focused blog writer.
  Create a high-ranking pillar blog for a local business.

Business Details:
  * Business Name : Urban Bites Café
  * Location      : Durban
  * Audience      : Students and young professionals
  * USP           : Affordable gourmet meals with fast service

SEO Requirements:
  * Primary keyword   : "Affordable café in Durban"
  * Secondary keywords: "Cheap eats Durban", "Best cafés Durban",
                        "Budget-friendly restaurants Durban"
  * Search intent     : Local + Transactional

Format Requirements:
  * 1000–1200 words
  * H1, H2, H3 structure
  * Strong hook in introduction
  * Problem → solution structure

Content Must Include:
  * Why affordability matters to the target audience
  * What customers struggle with — price vs quality trade-off
  * Why Urban Bites Café is the solution
  * Menu highlights with descriptive language
  * Experience — atmosphere, speed, and convenience
  * Internal links to all 4 supporting blogs
  * Strong CTA with urgency ("skip the wait", "visit today")

Tone:
  * Human and engaging
  * Modern — avoid robotic phrases
  * Optimised for mobile readability

 PROMPT 2: ADVANCED SUPPORTING BLOG PROMPT 
Role:
  Act as an SEO content writer specialising in local SEO.
  Write a supporting blog that targets a specific long-tail keyword.

Requirements:
  * 600–800 words per supporting blog
  * Clear H1, H2, H3 heading structure
  * Focus on one search intent — informational or local
  * Include Durban naturally throughout the content
  * Link back to the pillar blog
  * Strong CTA driving readers to Urban Bites Café
  * Helpful, specific, and practical — not generic


PROMPT 3: SEO CONTENT CLUSTER STRATEGY PROMPT
